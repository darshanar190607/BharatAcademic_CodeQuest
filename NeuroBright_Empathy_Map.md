## Empathy Map: NeuroBright Learner

**Target Persona**: A student or lifelong learner using the NeuroBright adaptive learning platform.
**Context**: The user is studying complex topics through the React web app while wearing an EEG-based emotion detection device that monitors their cognitive load, focus, and frustration levels in real time.

---

### 🗣️ SAYS
*What the user vocalizes out loud during or after the experience.*

* *"This lesson feels much easier to digest now that it slowed down."*
* *"I didn't even realize I was getting frustrated until the app suggested I take a 2-minute breather."*
* *"Who wants to collaborate on the next module? I've mastered this section."*
* *"The interface is really sleek, and it's cool seeing my focus stats."*
* *"I wonder if the headband actually knows I'm stressed or if it's just guessing."*
* *"I usually feel embarrassed when I don't get it right away, but the hints are really gentle here."*

### 💭 THINKS
*What occupies the user's internal monologue.*

* *"Is the EEG actually working? It genuinely seems to know when I zone out."*
* *"I hope my peers don't judge me if the system pairs me with someone for help."*
* *"This personalized approach makes studying feel less like a chore and more like a game."*
* *"Will my instructor/parents see that I was highly stressed during the math portion?"*
* *"I'm making real progress because the app isn't forcing me to move on before I'm ready."*
* *"I need to try and stay in the 'flow state' longer so I can clear this module."*

### 🏃 DOES
*The physical actions and behaviors the user performs.*

* **Wears hardware:** Puts on the EEG headset or headband before starting a study session.
* **Interacts smoothly:** Navigates the React-based web app, tracking personal "focus" and "emotion" metrics on their dashboard.
* **Follows cues:** Pauses or takes deep breaths when the system detects cognitive overload and prompts a break.
* **Collaborates:** Joins peer-to-peer breakout rooms or study groups when the platform identifies complimentary learning states among users.
* **Reflects:** Reviews their emotional timeline after a session to understand their own learning habits and peak performance times.

### ❤️ FEELS
*The emotional state of the user throughout their journey.*

* **Intrigued:** Fascinated by the cutting-edge technology and what it reveals about their own brain.
* **Relieved:** Grateful when the difficulty of a topic automatically scales down just as they start hitting a wall preventing burnout.
* **Motivated:** Driven by real-time positive feedback and rewards when they successfully maintain a state of 'flow'.
* **Self-conscious:** Initially slightly anxious or hyper-aware about wearing a device that "reads their mind" or emotions.
* **Empowered:** Confident, knowing the system caters strictly to their individual pace rather than a one-size-fits-all curriculum.
* **Supported:** Less lonely during remote learning, thanks to collaborative elements and non-judgmental, automated academic support.
